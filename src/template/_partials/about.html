About Us

Founders of The Vywamus Foundation

The Vywamus Foundation is a partnership among Vywamus, Patricia Marston-Snow and Halsey Snow, created for the purpose of providing individual and group transformational experiences and opportunities.

Founded in 1994, The Vywamus Foundation offers its programs and services in the U.S. and Europe, operating from offices near Portland, Maine at their educational center with several healing and intentional gardens, collectively referred to as... "A Place In The Heart."
